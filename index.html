About Khwancha Batteries

Accelerating Africa's transition to clean transportation

Our Vision

Khwancha Electrical Batteries Co. (Pty) Ltd is a battery technology startup focused on electric vehicle (EV) batteries. Our primary mission is to manufacture advanced lithium-ion batteries for EVs, aiming to serve as a homegrown supplier for Africa's emerging electric mobility market in South Africa.

Our long-term ambition is to build accelerating Africa's transition to clean transportation by developing local battery production capabilities and reducing reliance on imports.

We're positioning ourselves in the market as 'the EV battery company,' aiming to become synonymous with locally-made EV power solutions.

EV sales in South Africa remain a fraction of total vehicle sales, yet they have accelerated with more models available and rising fuel costs. This momentum continued into 2024 and is expected to grow.

Local production of EV batteries will be vital to support this growth and reduce reliance on imports. Khwancha is positioned to capture this emerging market with our locally manufactured battery solutions.

We're focusing on serving the growing demand from local EV manufacturers, commercial fleet operators, and EV conversion projects.

Our EV Battery Solutions

Advanced lithium-ion battery packs optimized for electric mobility

EV Battery Packs

Modular battery packs that power a range of EVs—from passenger cars and minibuses to commercial delivery vehicles.

Conversion Kits

Battery conversion kits and engineering support for EV retrofit projects, enabling combustion vehicles to be converted to electric drive.

Future Energy Solutions

Plans to expand into stationary energy storage, renewable energy integration, and battery recycling for a circular energy model.

Market Penetration Strategy

Targeting key early adopter segments and partnerships

Local EV Manufacturers

Partnering with South African OEMs to supply batteries for locally manufactured electric vehicles, reducing their reliance on imported components.

Commercial Fleet Operators

Targeting logistics/delivery companies, bus services, and corporate vehicle fleets that are experimenting with electrification to reduce costs.

EV Conversion Projects

Supporting enthusiast garages, tech startups, and academic institutions converting combustion vehicles to electric drive with custom battery solutions.
